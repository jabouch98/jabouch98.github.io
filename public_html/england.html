Dover Castle

Dover Castle is considered one of England's most iconic castles. It, of course if located in Dover England on the coast. Dover Castle is home to the Great Tower and underground tunnels that were used for battle.

Kenilworth Castle

Kenilworth Castle has been a cornerstone of English history for over 900 years. It is located in Kenilworth. It is home to beautiful tower views and a grand Great Hall.

Bolsover Castle

Bolsover Castle was built for entertainment. It is located in Derbyshire. You can do the wall walk in order to see the views or walk through the castle itself.
